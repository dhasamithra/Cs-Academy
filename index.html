Our management team brings a wealth of expertise in education. Our international curriculum and teaching methodology are proven and time tested. We have built on this vast experience and have developed a program that encompasses modern tools, but still adheres to traditional principles. Our boarding house is open for boys and girls from class IV. Both full boarding and week boarding options are available. Located at the base of the Western Ghats, the best school’s campus is picturesque and beautiful, ideally suited for exploring and learning.



At CS Academy we offer a number of programs through which learning extends beyond the traditional classroom. We believe in providing students the opportunity to learn from a variety of experiences.

The CS Academy schools are co-educational and are located in Tamil Nadu, India. We offer both CBSE and Cambridge at Coimbatore and CBSE in Erode. At our schools, we offer a high quality blend of co-curricular activities, sports and academics. Our team has extensive experience in the fields of K-XII education, corporate training, learning and development and career guidance both in India and abroad.

CS Academy recruits individuals who have the capacity to learn and grow with our organization. For the teachers who join us, we offer a compelling proposition – lunch subsidy, free transportation to key points in the city, a salary in line with the highest government pay scale, great quality education for their children and subsidy for school fees. Most importantly, the successful teacher will join the CS family – an organization that is experienced, yet dynamic and one which provides numerous growth opportunities.
